Jouni Helske

University of Jyväskylä, Department of Mathematics and Statistics, Finland, jouni.helske at jyu dot fi

I am an almost PhD in Statistics (estimated dissertation in October 2015) from University of Jyväskylä, Finland. My main interests are state space models, forecasting and R in all of its forms.

Selected R packages

  • Multivariate and Multichannel Discrete Hidden Markov Models for Categorical Sequences
  • Rlibeemd: Ensemble Empirical Mode Decomposition (EEMD) and Its Complete Variant (CEEMDAN)
