Jouni Helske

Linköping University, Department of Science and Technology (ITN), Media and Information Technology (MIT), Sweden, jouni.helske at liu dot se

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Linköping University, Sweden. My main research interests are state space models (hidden Markov models), Bayesian data analysis, and statistical software development.

Selected R packages

  • KFAS: Kalman Filter and Smoother for Exponential Family State Space Models
  • seqHMM: Hidden Markov Models for Life Sequences and Other Multivariate, Multichannel Categorical Time Series
  • bssm: Bayesian Inference of State Space Models
  • walker: Baysian Dynamic Regression Models with Stan
  • Rlibeemd: Ensemble Empirical Mode Decomposition (EEMD) and Its Complete Variant (CEEMDAN)
